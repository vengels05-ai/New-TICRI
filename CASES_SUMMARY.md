## Access the Cases

All cases are available at:
- `/cases/[category]/[case-name]`

For example:
- `/cases/foundational/marbury-v-madison`
- `/cases/civil-rights/brown-v-board-of-education-1954`
- `/cases/first-amendment/texas-v-johnson-1989`

Each case page includes:
- Year and category badge
- Full case citation
- Link to actual opinion (PDF)
- Summary of the opinion
- Why it mattered
- What it provided or took away
- Overreach or proper role analysis
- Plain-English impact today
- Fast facts (court, author, key clauses)
